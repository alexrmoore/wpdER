• Overall Aim – The aim of the escape room is to navigate through all the rooms, completing puzzles along the way to unlock further rooms/puzzles to reach the end in the shortest time possible.

  • Navigation – Navigate between rooms using the red up/down/left/right buttons which can be found at the edge of the game window. When viewing information/a puzzle, navigate back to the room using the back button in the upper left corner.

  • Information Points – Information to help you complete the puzzles can be found by clicking on the information points, as indicated by a book icon.

  • Puzzle Points – Puzzles that must be completed to progress can be accessed by clicking on the puzzle piece icons located in rooms across the escape room.

  • Traffic Light System – Incomplete puzzles are indicated by a red traffic light, whilst already completed puzzles are indicated by a green traffic light.

  • Locks – Locked rooms/puzzle pieces are indicated by a lock icon. These must be unlocked by completing a puzzle elsewhere in the escape room

  • End Screen – When the final room is reached, complete the escape room by clicking on the trophy.

  • In-game Instructions – Should you want to access the instructions at any point during the escape room, press this button in the bottom right corner of the screen.

  • Hints – Get stuck? Press the hint button in the bottom right corner of the screen. 3 hints are available for each puzzle, with a 30 second cooldown between each hint.

  • Give Up – Want to give up? Should you want to exit the game, please press the 'give-up' button in the bottom right of the screen, and continue on to complete the post-escape room survey.

  • Timer – You will have an infinite amount of time to complete this escape room. Nevertheless, a timer will be displayed in the bottom left of the screen so that you can track your progress.

  • Refresh/Back – Please note that the game will restart if you reload the page or press the browser's back button.
