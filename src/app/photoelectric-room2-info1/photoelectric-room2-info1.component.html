Discovery of the Photoelectric Effect
  • Heinrich Rudolf Hertz – 1887 – Observed that when ultraviolet light shines on two metal electrodes with a voltage applied across them, the light changes the voltage at which sparking takes place.


  • Phillip Lennard – 1902 – Demonstrated that electrically charged particles (electrons) are released from a metal surface when it is illuminated.


  • Albert Einstein – 1905 – Used the phenomena observed when investigating the photoelectric effect to theorise about light quanta (i.e. particles of light).
