The Photon
  • A packet or bundle of energy is called a
    photon.

  • Namely, a photon is a packet/quanta of
    electromagnetic radiation, and is a type of
    elementary particle.

  • A photon can be considered to be a 'particle of light'

  • A photon travels at the speed of light, c, in a vacuum

  • A photon has zero rest mass (i.e. cannot exist at rest), so must be moving at all times
