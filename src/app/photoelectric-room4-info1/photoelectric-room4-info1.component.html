The Photon

A packet or bundle of energy is called a photon.
Namely, a photon is a packet/quanta of electromagnetic
radiation, and is a type of elementary particle.

Energy of a photon is: E = hf = hc/lambda
(where h is the Planckâ€™s constant, f is the frequency of the radiation/photon, c is the speed of light, and lambda is the wavelength of the radiation)

A photon travels at the speed of light, c, in a vacuum

A photon has zero rest mass (i.e. cannot exist at rest)
